Travel Safety Tips

Practical tips to keep your trip smooth and secure.

  • Carry digital and physical copies of important documents (ID, tickets, insurance).
  • Share your itinerary and register your trip with SafeVoyage before departure.
  • Avoid isolated areas at night; prefer well-lit, busy routes.
  • Use verified transport & hotel services; confirm bookings and reviews.
  • Keep emergency numbers handy (tourist helpline: 1800-11-1363).
  • Follow local advisories and official guidance for the destination.

Sources: Ministry of Tourism guidelines and official travel advisories.

About Us

SafeVoyage uses AI, geo-fencing and blockchain-backed digital IDs to monitor registered trips and accelerate incident response. We prioritise privacy and security: registrations are encrypted client→server (in production), access is restricted to authorised responders, and data is used only for safety & incident management.

This demo is front-end only; production must implement secure backends, encryption, OTP-based verification and legal data handling for Aadhaar & PII.
